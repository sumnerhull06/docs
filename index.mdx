The RepairDesk Authorize.Net Bridge is a self-hosted Node.js service that fills the gap between RepairDesk's invoice management and Authorize.Net's Accept Hosted checkout. When a customer pays, the bridge receives the Authorize.Net webhook and automatically records the payment against the correct RepairDesk invoice — no manual entry required, and card data never touches your server.

## Get up and running

Follow these four steps to go from zero to accepting payments through Authorize.Net inside RepairDesk.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the bridge">
    Clone the repository and install its dependencies with `npm install`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure environment">
    Copy `.env.example` to `.env` and fill in your RepairDesk API key, Authorize.Net credentials, and the public URL where your bridge is hosted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up the Authorize.Net webhook">
    Point an Authorize.Net webhook at `POST /webhooks/authorize-net` on your bridge so that approved transactions are automatically recorded in RepairDesk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the Chrome extension or userscript">
    Load the bundled Chrome extension (or add the Tampermonkey userscript) so staff see an **Authorize.Net Pay** button directly inside RepairDesk POS.
  </Step>
</Steps>
